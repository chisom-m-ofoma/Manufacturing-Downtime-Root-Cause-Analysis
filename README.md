# Manufacturing-Process-Efficiency-Analysis

## Business Overview

Delcrone is a manufacturing company that uses the industrial Internet of Things (IoT) to track technology data from its factories. The tech team at Delcrone has collected and converted a month's worth of telemetry data from their four (4) production facilities, and the stakeholders would like insight from this converted data to identify the biggest problem disrupting the assembly line. Each production facility has 9 types of machines that send a message every 10 minutes.

### Key Metrics
- In which facility do machines break the most?
- What are the machines that broke most often in that facility?

## Analysis

- Converted data (JSON file) was imported into Tableau and a calculated field (Unhealthy) was created the determine how long the downtime of the pieces of the manufacturing processes were.
